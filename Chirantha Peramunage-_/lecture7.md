
### RGB light 
* d("rgb_multi", "rgb1", d7, 10) port, number of leds you want to activate.
* run()
* mqtt_send rgb1/set on 
* mqtt_send rgb1/rgb/set 2 blue
* mqtt_send rgb1/rgb/set red
